# Task 4: Professional Communication & Ethics 📧

This folder contains my formal email drafts and a professional social media conduct guide.

### 📜 Social Media Code of Conduct (10-Point Checklist)
1. *Think Before Posting:* Evaluate if the content is helpful or necessary.
2. *Verify Sources:* Avoid spreading unverified news or rumors.
3. *Respect Privacy:* Do not share others' information without consent.
4. *Professional Tone:* Use respectful language in all digital interactions.
5. *Protect Personal Data:* Never post sensitive IDs or contact details.
6. *No Cyberbullying:* Report and block harassment immediately.
7. *Give Credit:* Cite original authors when sharing content.
8. *Secure Accounts:* Use strong passwords and enable 2FA.
9. *Digital Footprint:* Only post what is appropriate for future employers.
10. *Inclusivity:* Be respectful of all cultures and backgrounds.
