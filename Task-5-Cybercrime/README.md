# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention 🛡️

This folder contains research and actionable safety measures regarding digital threats and phishing.

### 📂 Folder Contents:
* *Case Study:* casestudy-phishing.pdf (Analysis of the Amanda Todd case)
* *Prevention Guide:* safety-checklist.pdf (10-point "Stay Safe Online" guide)
* *README.md:* Folder summary and core prevention tips.

### 🛡️ Quick Prevention Tips:
1. *Enable 2FA:* Always use Two-Factor Authentication on all accounts.
2. *Privacy Settings:* Keep your social media profiles private.
3. *Report & Block:* Never engage with phishing ; document and report them.
4. *Link Caution:* Never click on suspicious links in emails or SMS (Phishing).

### 🎯 Objective:
To analyze the impact of cybercrime on students and promote a culture of digital safety and resilience.
